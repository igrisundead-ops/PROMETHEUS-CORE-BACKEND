# Nolan clip-cutting reference script
# One idea per line or use "Label: text" pairs.

Hook: Open with a concrete payoff, result, or surprising lesson in the first breath.
Retention: Highlight where editing, structure, or pacing changed audience retention.
Transformation: Show a before-and-after shift in skill, business, confidence, or results.
Specificity: Favor numbers, timelines, percentages, or concrete details over generic claims.
Payoff: End right after the key lesson lands so the clip feels complete and leaves curiosity.
